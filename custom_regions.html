List of used custom regions

Some notes:
  • This list shows only assigned, active or permanent reserved regions. Temporary reserved regions are not listed.
  • Each region, that was never used, becomes candidate for an automatic disabling procedure 12 months after assignment. Each region, that was not used in the last 24 months, becomes candidate too. In both cases, the owner gets a warning message.

    The timeout of 24 months is increased to 3, 4 or 5 years if at least 250, 1000 or 3000 matches were played in the region.

    31 days after the warning message, the owner loses the control of the region, gets a second message and the region is disabled. From now on the owner has a chance to re-apply the region. 31 days later, the region is hidden from the lists.

    12 months after hiding, but not earlier than 24 months after last usage, the region is released and goes back into the pool of available regions.

  • The match counters are active since August 2016. Since August 2017, matches in private rooms are counted too, but only if main.dol  is patched to send the region number at login time.
  • Click »Owner« to hide regions of other owners. Click again to show all regions again.
